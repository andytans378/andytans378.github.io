Project Idea

My project idea is basically a contact lens that anybody can wear that allows the user to have a user interface in reality. In other words the user will have an interface over the real world allowing them to access things such as google maps, time, calendar, reminders and so many more apps and widgets without looking at their phone. I mean just think about it, the government is currently working on stopping people from using their phones on the road, but if notifications and maps were apart of their vision, drivers would not have to look down at their phones to look at the map or see who is calling them. Rather it will come up in their prereferral vision whilst driving. This project is especially appealing to me as I have seen it in a number of different movies where users are able to see a virtual reality when they put on these lenses. In the Korean drama, Memories of Alhambra, the protagonist find himself in charge of contact lenses that immerses himself into a kind of virtual reality game where the game world is the real world. I found this idea quite intriguing the first time I saw it, so I figured a good project idea would be a contact lens that could be applied to our daily lives to help us in different ways. The contact lenses will be sold in a case that contains a jellylike substance that retains moisture in the lens. When the lens is placed on the eyes like any normal contact lens. The lens will then detect the user by doing a retina scan to verify identity. The lens will then open up this futuristic display that can only be seen by the user due to the lens. From this display the user can select things and move thigs around by moving their hands in front of their eyes, like a tablet. The lens will scan the movement and adjust the interface accordingly. The user will have access to applications such as google maps and new virtual reality games that are supported on their phone’s platform. They will also have access to widgets that they can customize on their interface using their hands. Due to the unique way the lens work, each user can create an interface according to their likes and needs. Applications and widgets can be kept open whilst moving about to help users in their day to day life. These applications will also work with Siri or Cortana or the equivalent AI that their phone’s platform supports. Therefore on the day to day bases, users will be able to have functions such as google maps in front of them, be able to see notifications such as texts or who is currently calling them; due to the unique hand movements that can be scanned using the lens, users will also have complete control over these functions allowing them to accept and hang up on phone calls, move the map around to be able to see their surroundings. This is only the beginning of the potential that’s these lens’ can allow. With this revolutionary piece of technology, we can erase hazards such as drivers on their phones and pedestrians on their phones. Furthermore, this technology can be the start of a new revolution. Being an apple fanatic, I will work to have this new technology support apple products. The user interface will be able to be linked with devices such as air pods, iPhone’s, apple watches and apple home to allow for control from the user interface. Functions such as being able to skip music, pause and control volume; functions which the air pods are missing alone, will be possible with these lenses. When needed the user will be able to turn off the interface to show normal vision of the standard real world. This will allow full control over the interface to stop distractions when needed. These lenses will also be able to come with a prescription with special order allowing people with imperfect vision to utilise contact lenses with this new technology. These require special order the lens will have to be adjusted to suit the prescription of the user. This allows any person to be able to find the right lenses for them.
